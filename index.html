About Us

The Trackside Restaurant

Welcome to Trackside Restaurant. A Taste of Portugal, Since 2005

Trackside offers a taste of authentic Portuguese cuisine rooted in traditional recipes and fresh, seasonal ingredients. Delight in the flavors and aromas of Portuguese cuisine while you listen to Portuguese music and enjoy a glass of Portuguese wine. Enjoy a wonderful dining experience in a warm and casual atmosphere. You are certain to fall in love with our cuisine and we will rejoice when you return time and time again.
